Services

Chances are, if you clicked on this page, you're curious what I can do for you. Maybe you're a small-business owner and you need a website to attract new customers. Or perhaps you're an arist who wants a snazzy portfolio to land gigs. Whatever your situation may be, if you need a website, I can help.

But Kristi, there are a million free website creators out there! Why do I need you?

Indeed, there are! And you can use one, if you want. But unless you have the time or technical know-how to customize its look and feel, your design will be determined by the templates they offer. You want to impress people, right? Don't let an out-of-the-box look get in the way of your one-of-a-kind business.

So, what can you do for me?

So glad you asked!

If you already have a website but it needs an update, I can go in and redesign it for you. If you don't have a website at all, no problem! We'll work together to come up with content that catches your audience's eye and brings them into your business.

In either case, the details are up to you! I can create a "static" website, one that doesn't require much updating, or I can build it on Wordpress so that you can easily go in and update it yourself. Your choice! I code everything from scratch, so there is no detail that I won't agonize over on your behalf!

Best of all, all services come with Search Engine Optimization (SEO), so you can be sure your new website will appear when your potential clients search for you.
