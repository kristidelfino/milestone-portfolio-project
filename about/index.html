About Me

So, you want to know more about me?

Well, let me tell you, it's a doozy! It's really not...

I started my professional life as a teacher. I've taught Spanish and French to hundreds, maybe even thousands, of students. And while I enjoyed the job, something never felt quite right. As someone once put it, it was like wearing a shoe that didn't quite fit. Sure, you could bear it for a while, but eventually it starts to chafe.

So, after 11 years in the classroom, I decided to hang up the ol' apple* (why is that a teacher thing, anyway?) and try my hand at learning new skills. I signed up for Skillcrush, and here I am! *I actually do still teach college courses part time. What can I say, I enjoy it!

But don't think this is something completely new! I grew up among that first generation of full-time internet users. I coded a billion websites way back in the old days of Geocities and Angelfire. The fact that I fell into it again as an adult just feels like I've come full circle! Like I've landed home.
